Know BobNet

Any website using .bob or link.bob is by Freakybob Team. If you are using the official BobNet client, these will ALWAYS be by us.

What does link.bob do? Its a service that allows us to link to external websites not on BobNet (such as Freakybob.site)!

What do .bob domains do? They are official BobNet pages that can contain support and information about BobNet.
